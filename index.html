Apple scraps

Apple scraps - Ferment your own vinegar

Apple scrap vinegar. Combine the peel and cores from 6 large apples, 1 tbsp of sugar and enough water to cover in a bowl. Stir and cover with a thin cloth. Over the next several days, stir your fermentation several times a day. Once the ferment starts bubbling, stir it once a day. Fermentation times vary, but your vinegar will likely start to taste sour after about a week. When it stops fizzing completely, you can store it in bottles or jars. Be sure to burp them (open to release gas) once in a while incase it's still fermenting a little!
